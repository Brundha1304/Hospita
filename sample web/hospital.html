Welcome to Apollo Hospital,Chennai

Apollo Hospitals provide world-class healthcare facilities equipped with cutting-edge technology, including advanced operating theatres, intensive care units (ICUs), specialized transplant centers, diagnostic imaging (MRI, CT, PET-CT), and comfortable inpatient rooms. The hospital is designed to offer comprehensive medical services under one roof, ensuring quality care and patient comfort. In emergencies, Apollo offers 24/7 critical care through its well-equipped Accident & Emergency Department, featuring rapid response ambulances, dedicated trauma teams, advanced life-support systems, and tele-ICU services—delivering immediate, expert attention in life-threatening situations.For emergencies, Apollo has a 24x7 Accident & Emergency Department with trained trauma teams, rapid response ambulances, tele-ICU support, and life-saving equipment. They are capable of handling critical conditions like cardiac arrest, stroke, trauma, and poisoning, ensuring immediate and expert care.
